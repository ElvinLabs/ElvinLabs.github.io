Why are we building it and whom is it for

If you are a person who is using a prepaid mobile connection in Sri Lanka and use recharge cards to reload your connection, this app is for you.

When someone needs to reload using recharge cards he or she needs to type down all the numbers in that recharge card with appropriate USSD code. For example, User has to type 14 numbers from the card and another 6 characters. All together it is 20 characters. Most of the time it is really annoying to type them down.

But if you use this app to reload mobile this app will do all those things for you. You just have to open up the app and detect recharge card using app camera and give command to recharge after recognizing the card. App will add the appropriate USSD codes and fill up the card numbers for you.
