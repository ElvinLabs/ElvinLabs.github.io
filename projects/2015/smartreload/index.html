Why we're building it... and whom it's for

If you're a person using a prepaid connection (in Sri Lanka) using recharge-cards, this is the app for you.

As a recharge-card user, you will have to type down all the 14 numbers in it in USSD or in a machine-voiced call, with extra keypresses, which is really annoying... not to add the frustration when you type it all wrong.

But what if you had an app that does exactly that for you... and does it better? You'll just have to open up the app from an easily-accessible shortcut and use the camera to detect the number on the card... and the rest is history.
